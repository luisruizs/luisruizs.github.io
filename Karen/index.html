Biography

Hi I'm Karen

I'm a photographer with a style that is romantic and vibrant. I strive most for my work to echo the emotion of the moment.

I look forward to capturing the natural beauty of your family, the magic of your pregnancy, the special moments with your newborn, the smiles of your children, and the romance shared with your partner. I am inspired by the locations I shoot in and the people I meet.
